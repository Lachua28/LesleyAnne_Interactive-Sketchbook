Micro-Project: Progress #2

1. Current Build (Brief Description)

In this version of my system—which I’m going to call a “game” from now on—I have added pretty much all of the game mechanics, so everything works as intended.

My main variables are “busX” and “stopX”, which determine where the bus and bus stop are. The main change is that the player clicks the screen to stop the bus, which has automated movement. If the player clicks the screen while busX and stopX are overlapping, they win the level. Then, if the player presses a key while on the “win” or “fail” states, they move on to the next level or reset, respectively.

2. Where Is the Pressure?

The tension in my system is caused by speed escalation and accumulation with each level. The bus’s speed is the frame count multiplied by the “currentLevel” variable. The currentLevel goes up by 1 with each win (every time the “win” variable is turned to “true”), so the speed goes up with every level win.
