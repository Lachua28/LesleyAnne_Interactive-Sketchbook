Micro-Project Idea Bank

Waiting for the Bus

Issue: After waiting for the bus, you either get on or miss the bus.

Interaction rule: The input would be pressing a button to board the bus when the bus is at a specific point/stop. The change over time is the bus moving and reaching then passing a bus stop; I would like to use automated movement over time if we learn that! The delay would be waiting for the bus to reach a certain point.

Intended feeling: Waiting & anticipation

"Some people have it easy, others have to work hard"

System: You get a reward for clicking a certain amount of times.

Interaction rule: The input is clicks or button presses. The change over time would be some sort of meter going up, but a random determination at the start requires you to either need a lot or a little amount of clicks to fill the meter. Friction appears in the beginning where its determined whether you need to click more than the "easy" path.

Intended feeling: frustration and privilege, depending on your luck

Growing a Plant

Behaviour: You grow a plant over time by doing a certain action over time.

Interaction rule: The input will either be a click/press on at least 2 different buttons. The change over time is the plant growing. The limit or uneveness would either be that each action only grows the plant until a certain point, or that you have to wait for the plant to grow.

Intended feeling: waiting/patience
